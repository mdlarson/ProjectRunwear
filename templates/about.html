About Project Runwear

I created this site because I often can't remember what weather feels like, especially when I'm running and working up a sweat. And the problem got worse when this Atlanta moved to Brooklyn, and had to figure out what works in longer, colder winters! I previously kept a note on my phone with outfits at each temperature range, which I would consult after checking my local weather. I created this site to save myself a step. :) I'm indebted to ___, ___, ___, ___, and ___ for their perspective and clothing recommendations.
