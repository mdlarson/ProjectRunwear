About Project Runwear

I created this site because I often can't remember what weather feels like, especially when I'm running and working up a sweat. And the problem got worse when this Atlantan moved to Brooklyn, and had to figure out what works well for longer, colder winters! I previously kept a note on my phone with outfits at each temperature range, which I would consult after checking my local weather. I created this site to save myself a step. :)

I'm particularly grateful for winter clothing recommendations from Tina Muir and Amanda Brooks. Their advice helped me discern my favorite outfits. And Dress My Run was a clear and useful inspiration for this more personal version.
